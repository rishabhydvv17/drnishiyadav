About the Doctor

Dr. Nishi Yadav
MBBS, MD (Dermatology, Venereology & Leprosy)

Trained at one of India's prestigious medical institutions, Dr. Nishi Yadav brings over a decade of clinical and surgical expertise to every patient she treats. Her approach combines cutting-edge cosmetic technology with genuine care for long-term skin health.

After completing her MD in Dermatology, Venereology & Leprosy, she specialized in advanced dermatosurgery and lasers. Her fellowship training in laser systems and aesthetic procedures allows her to deliver medical-grade rejuvenation protocols with artistic precision.

She founded the Etawah clinic with the conviction that patients in the region deserve the same world-class, scientifically proven dermatological care available in major metropolitan centers.

My Philosophy

Why I Practice

Dermatology, for me, has always been about the person sitting across the table, not just the condition they present. Every patient who walks into our clinic comes with a story, an insecurity, or a hope. My responsibility is to honor that with an honest assessment and a plan that truly serves their skin's long-term health, not just a quick cosmetic fix.

I chose to establish my practice in Etawah because I believe world-class dermatology should not be the exclusive privilege of those living in major metropolitan cities. The science is the same, the standards must be the same, and the respect for every patient must be absolute.

That philosophy drives every consultation and treatment choice. I will never recommend a procedure you don't need, and I will always explain what is clinically realistic. That honesty is the foundation our clinic is built on.

"I believe every patient deserves a doctor who tells the truth, not just what they want to hear."

Dr. Nishi Yadav
